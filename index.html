The Challenge

AI Systems Create Attack Surfaces Traditional Security Can't Handle

Your organization is deploying AI faster than security teams can adapt. Legacy playbooks weren't designed for machine learning threats—leaving critical gaps attackers exploit.

Model Poisoning

Attackers corrupt training data, causing AI models to make dangerous, biased, or malicious decisions at scale.

Prompt Injection

Malicious inputs manipulate LLMs into bypassing safeguards, leaking data, or executing unauthorized actions.

Adversarial Attacks

Subtle input perturbations cause catastrophic misclassifications—invisible to humans, devastating to AI systems.
